The bar chart below illustrates the annual number of ransomware attacks worldwide (in millions) from 2016 to 2020. The horizontal axis represents the year,while the vertical axis represents the number of ransomware attacks. It can be seen that the number of ransomware attacks fell extremly from 638 million in 2016 to 184 million in 2017. The Numbers of ransomware attacks in 2018 (204,24 million) and 2019 (187,9 million) are relatively stable, but with 304 million ransomware attacks in 2020 we can see a clear and alarming upward trend in the annual number of ransomware attacks.
